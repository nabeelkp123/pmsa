Our kerala


  • Kerala is a state on the south-western side of India

  • The total geographical area of Kerala is 38,863 square kilometres

  • Kerala is the 21st largest in the area and 13th largest state in population.

  • It was formed on 1st November 1959, Following the passage of the states Reorganisation Act

  • Karnataka borders it to the north and northeast, Tamil Nadu to The east and south, and The Lakshadweep Sea to the west
